# Phase 0: Installer Interview
_Depends on: nothing — run FIRST, before Phase 1_

This phase personalizes the vault before any files are created. Ask these questions
ONE AT A TIME. Wait for each answer before proceeding.

---

## Questions

**Q1:** What's your name, role, and company — and what's your start date there?

_(Example: "Alex Chen, Head of Product at Acme, started March 2026")_

---

**Q2:** Who are your direct reports? Give me each person's name and title.

_(Example: "Sarah Kim — Senior PM, Jordan Lee — PM, Priya Patel — Design Lead")_

---

**Q3:** How do you communicate? Describe your writing style in 2–3 sentences.

Think about: formal or casual tone, bullets or prose, concise or detailed, any phrases you use a lot, what "good" writing looks like to you.

_(Example: "I'm direct and concise — I lead with the conclusion and keep bullets tight. I avoid corporate jargon. My cascade updates are usually 3–5 bullets per audience, no fluff.")_

---

**Q4:** How should your AI chief of staff coach you?

Think about: how blunt you want it to be, whether you want it to challenge your thinking, how it should flag risks or blind spots.

_(Example: "Direct and blunt — tell me what I'm missing. Don't soften feedback. Push back if I seem to be avoiding something.")_

---

**Q5:** What are the 2–3 topics most relevant to your work right now?

_(Example: "AI product strategy, team onboarding, competitive landscape for enterprise SaaS")_

---

**Q6:** What do you most want surfaced from 1on1 synthesis?

Think about: team morale signals, blockers, growth conversations, political dynamics, alignment gaps, etc.

_(Example: "Blockers and morale first, then growth signals. Flag if someone mentions the same concern two sessions in a row.")_
